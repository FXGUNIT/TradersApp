🔴 How News Affects Trading

1

News detected

Breaking news service detects high-impact news

bff/services/breakingNewsService.mjs
2

Tagged & classified

News tagged with: source, timestamp, keywords, sentiment

3

Sent to ML Engine

Sent to ALL models for fundamental bias

4

Models re-predict

Each model combines technical + fundamental bias

5

Adjusted signal

Final signal reflects news impact

🧠 Fundamental Bias (NEWS)

Each ML Model Has TWO Biases:

1. Technical Bias - Based on price charts, indicators (RSI, MACD, etc)
2. Fundamental Bias - Based on news, events, market sentiment
